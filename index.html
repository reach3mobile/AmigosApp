How to save NM Amigo Itinerary app icon to your home screen on the top 5 smart phones.

OS (iPhone, iPod, iPad):Adding app bookmark icon to home screen.

1. Touch the box/arrow or the + icon that's at the bottom of the screen, in the center of the bottom bar. Note: iPads will have this icon on the top of the window.

2. You should get a list of options that pops up.

3. Pick "Add to Home Screen"

4. On the next screen, type in the name you want to use for the app and click the "add" button

5. It should now appear alongside your other apps on your home screen.

BlackBerry: Adding app bookmark icon to home screen.

Press the "BlackBerry" key to bring up the pop-up menu and select "Add to Home Screen" from that menu.

1. Type in the name of the bookmark in the first text box on the screen. This box will default to the name of the page, so you don't have to change it if you don't want to.

2. Choose the folder that you want to save the bookmark to by selecting the box next to "Location" and choosing your desired folder from the drop-down menu. Press "Add" when you are ready to add the bookmark. Your bookmark will now be an icon on your home screen, or in the folder you selected.


HTC Rezound: Adding app bookmark.

If you are not already on the Home screen, tap the Home key.

1. Tap Menu.

2. Tap Add.

3. Tap Add bookmark.

4. Enter the name of the bookmark, NM Amigos.

5. Enter the location the bookmark should go to. Tap Done.

The bookmark is now saved.

Add bookmark to home screen

1. From the Home screen, select the menu icon.

2. Select Personalize.

3. Select Shortcut.

4. Select bookmark.

5. Select NM Amigos shortcut.


Android: Adding app bookmark icon to home screen.

Follow these steps to add a URL bookmark icon to your Android's home screen:

1. Bookmark the desired page from your phone's default browser.

2. From your Android's home screen, where you would like to add the bookmark icon, long-press (press and hold unoccupied space with your finger for about 2 seconds).

3. From "Add to Home Screen", select "Shortcuts" and then select "Bookmark".?

4. Select the desired bookmark and BOOM, you now have a bookmark icon shortcut on your Android's home screen!

How to use your app

Home screen:

-Day 1, this screen will have a welcome message with these directions and a short welcome message.

-Everyday, this screen will have buttons to your Itinerary, Quiz, NM (past, present and future photos page) and the amigo of the day bio page.

NOTE: you can access this screen only through launching the app. To navigate the site on other pages use the bottom NAVIGATION bar.

Itinerary Page:

-Day 1, Trip information button on top of itinerary.

-Top of app, use the "Next" and "Prev" buttons to navigate the days.

-After times, you will sometimes see a "x" or "xx" these are the required cloths for the particular event. Click on the x's for details.

Quiz:

-You will have access to five quizzes a day. You can look back at old quizzes via the "prev" button. You will not be able to skip ahead.

Bio Page:

-You will receive a new bio pic daily. You will not be able to see future bios but you can see previous bios via the "Prev" button.

NM Page:

-You will receive a new NM pic daily. You will not be able to see future pics but you can see previous bios via the "Prev" button.

Note: on new smart phones you can rotate your device to see a larger version of the pic.
